# Petal Apothecary

**Adapter card:** <ItemLink id="ae2ltpp:botania_petal_apothecary_adapter" />
**Mod:** Botania

Automates Petal Apothecary recipes (petals + a seed → a mystical flower). Place the provider against the
Apothecary and encode the recipe as a pattern. There is no crafting delay; the result is returned right
away.

## ⚠ Water per craft

Each craft consumes **one charge of water** from the Apothecary, just like crafting by hand. Either keep
the basin filled with water, or include a **water source** (e.g. a water bucket) in the pattern's inputs
so the adapter can supply it. The seed/reagent is a normal ingredient and is consumed.
