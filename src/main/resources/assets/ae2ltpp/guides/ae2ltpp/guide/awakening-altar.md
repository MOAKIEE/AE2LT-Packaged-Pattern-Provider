# Awakening Altar

**Packaged Core:** <ItemLink id="ae2ltpp:ma_awakening_altar_packaged_core" />
**Mod:** Mystical Agriculture

Automates Awakening Altar recipes. Place the provider against the Awakening Altar and encode the recipe
as a pattern. The adapter places the items and returns the result.

## ⚠ Pedestals, Vessels & Essence

The recipe needs the 8 surrounding positions to be exactly **4 Awakening Pedestals + 4 Essence Vessels**
(their arrangement among the 8 spots doesn't matter).

Encode the recipe with the altar item, the 4 pedestal items, and the 4 required essence stacks. The
adapter fills the empty Essence Vessels automatically (each holds up to 40), places the other items,
then starts the craft.
