# Hephaestus Forge

**Adapter card:** <ItemLink id="ae2ltpp:fa_hephaestus_forge_adapter" />
**Mod:** Forbidden Arcanus

Automates Hephaestus Forge rituals. Place the provider against the Forge. The adapter puts the main
ingredient into the Forge and the rest onto the surrounding pedestals, then starts the ritual and pulls
the result back when it finishes (a ritual has a fixed duration with the usual magic-circle effect).

## Structure

* Up to **8 Pedestals** within about 4 blocks of the Forge (the usual Forge setup).

## ⚠ You must keep Essence stocked

Rituals cost **essence** (Aureal / Souls / Blood / Experience). This adapter **does not supply essence**
— it only spends what the Forge already holds. Fill the Forge's essence through Forbidden Arcanus' own
means (e.g. its essence pickup), or the ritual will wait until enough essence is available.
