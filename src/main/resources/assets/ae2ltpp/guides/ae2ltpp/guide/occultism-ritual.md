# Occultism Ritual

**Adapter card:** <ItemLink id="ae2ltpp:occultism_ritual_adapter" />
**Mod:** Occultism

Automates Occultism rituals. Place the provider against the **Golden Sacrificial Bowl** that starts the
ritual. The adapter drops the ritual's main item into the bowl and the ingredients onto the surrounding
sacrificial bowls, then lets the ritual run.

## ⚠ Set up the ritual first

* You must have the full ritual ready: the correct **pentacle drawn** and the **sacrificial bowls** placed
  around the Golden Sacrificial Bowl, exactly as the ritual requires.
* Rituals take in-world time and have visible effects; the adapter automates the item placement (and can
  proxy the sacrifice / item-use cost), but the ritual itself still plays out normally.
