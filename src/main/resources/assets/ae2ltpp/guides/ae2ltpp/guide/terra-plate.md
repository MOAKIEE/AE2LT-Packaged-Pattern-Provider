# Terra Plate (Terrestrial Agglomeration Plate)

**Packaged Core:** <ItemLink id="ae2ltpp:botania_terra_plate_packaged_core" />
**Mod:** Botania

Automates Terrestrial Agglomeration (e.g. fusing Terrasteel from Manasteel, Mana Pearl and Mana Diamond).
Place the provider against the Terra Plate and encode the recipe as a pattern. The adapter feeds the
items; once the plate has gathered enough mana it produces the output, which is returned.

## ⚠ Mana

* The plate consumes a large amount of **mana** from surrounding mana sources, and there is an in-world
  delay while it charges. Keep mana flowing in or the craft will simply take longer.
