# Alfheim Portal

**Packaged Core:** <ItemLink id="ae2ltpp:botania_alfheim_portal_packaged_core" />
**Mod:** Botania

Automates Elven Trade recipes through the Alfheim Portal. Place the provider against the portal frame
and encode the trade as a pattern; the traded items are returned.

## ⚠ Keep the portal open

* The trade only runs while the **portal is open** (activated). Make sure it stays open during automation.
* One trade is processed per request. For volume, submit the pattern multiple times rather than expecting
  a single large batch.
